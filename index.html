01

Problem

Forearm amputees face a critical gap: affordable, functional prosthetics simply don't exist at scale. Current options force a painful tradeoff.


Pain points:


→ Bionic arms cost $10K–$100K+, out of reach for most


→ Mechanical hooks are cheap but offer minimal dexterity


→ Developing world amputees have virtually no access to advanced devices


→ Social stigma compounds functional limitation
